WELCOME

“Monuments By Effie” is a family owned and operated headstone company located in New York City. Our goal is to treat every family as our own. We understand that our services are needed during your time of need. Our prospective goal is to extend our services to help every family during their difficult time, when purchasing a headstone for your loved one.
